About


The International Symposium on Biomimetic Intelligence and Robotics (ISBIR) is a conference that presents the key achievements, current frontiers, and future prospects of biomimetic robots and artificial intelligence. Over the past decade, we have witnessed the rapid development of science and technologies in biomimetic intelligence and smart agents designed for various scenarios, such as medical robots and service robots. ISBIR provides a great platform for the world's leading researchers, educators, industry experts, and academics in relevant fields to share experiences and insights in terms of solutions to bottlenecks as well as advances in performance.

ISBIR is an affiliated conference of the journal, Biomimetic Intelligence and Robotics (BIROB) sponsored by Shandong University. It serves as an international platform for introducing and promoting the journal, including plenary/keynote speeches and technical sessions to share the latest breakthrough in biomimetic intelligence and robotics, international forums for related hot research topics, and workshops to introduce the detailed research methods to young scholars.

Submission


1. Download the Elsevier Latex Template. All papers should follow Double-column style. More infomation about Latex template can be found at this link.


2. The page limit is 6 pages for the paper, including text and figures, + 1 page for the bibliography/references.


3. Submit your paper via ISBIR 2022 at Microsoft CMT.


Accepted papers presented at the conference will be submitted for inclusion into Procedia Computer Science, which is indexed in Scopus, the Web of Science Conference Proceedings Citation Index, INSPEC, and Engineering Village.


Expanded versions of the accepted papers with high quality will be invited for publication in Biomimetic Intelligence and Robotics.
